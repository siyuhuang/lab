Prospective Students

  • Prospective PhD students: We are looking for motivated PhD students, particularly those from underrepresented groups, to join our lab. Please apply to Clemson CS PhD program and indicate your interest in working with Dr. Siyu Huang. Feel free to email Dr. Siyu Huang (siyuh@clemson.edu) your CV with "PhD application - Your name" as the subject.
  • Current Clemson undergraduate and graduate students:Clemson undergraduate and graduate students, particularly those from underrepresented groups, are encouraged to reach out to our lab members for discussions on any collaborations. We are open to collaborations both inside and outside the School of Computing at Clemson University.
  • Visiting students and research interns at external institutes: We are looking for visiting students and research interns! Please email Dr. Siyu Huang (siyuh@clemson.edu) your CV with "Intern application - Your name" as the subject.
